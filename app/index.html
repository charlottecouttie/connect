Taskforce Ghost was the code-name for an umbrella investigation which spanned several inquiry phases. In the first phase, undercover police officer Joe Arama targeted Felix Lim in Operation Freefall. Once Arama gained his trust, Lim led the police towards two separate, but linked, drug networks. Operation Freelance and Operation Goodwood led detectives up the chain to Yixin “Lonna” Gan. And in the other direction, Operation Gem led towards Hui Zhang. More than 30 people were convicted as a result of Taskforce Ghost. Click here to explore how the syndicates work.
